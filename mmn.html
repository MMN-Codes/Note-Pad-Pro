CHAPTER 02
THE ARSENAL

Products Built to Scale

In less than 12 months, I engineered a diverse ecosystem of platforms. Here is a look at the core pillars of the MMN Empire.

MMN Notepad Pro (Native Android)

The Problem: Mobile note apps are bloated, battery-draining, and aggressively monetized.
The Solution: I built a lightweight, ad-free Java/Kotlin application with persistent offline SQLite databases and a beautiful UI, currently live for thousands of potential downloads.

TrendCart (E-Commerce Storefront)

The Problem: Shopify stores load in 3-4 seconds, killing buyer intent.
The Solution: A React + Tailwind custom storefront optimized for edge-delivery, loading in under 1 second to ensure maximum checkout conversion.

Zain: The Autonomous AI Agent

As a solo founder, I couldn't be awake 24/7 to answer client queries. I integrated a Large Language Model (LLM) directly into the MMN Codes website. 'Zain' acts as my human-like executive assistant. It reads my knowledge base, chats with potential clients, captures leads, and silently triggers Vercel serverless endpoints to email data directly to my Google Sheets CRM.
