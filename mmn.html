The Portfolio
02

The MMN Ecosystem (1/2)

I didn't just build one type of application. To prove my full-stack capabilities, I tackled different industries: Mobile Apps, E-Commerce, and EdTech.

Android App

📱 MMN Notepad Pro

The Problem: The Google Play Store is flooded with note-taking apps that are heavy, slow, and packed with intrusive ads that ruin the user experience and drain battery.

The Solution: I engineered a native Android application focused entirely on premium productivity. It features a lightweight ad-free experience, a local SQLite database for lightning-fast offline search, and seamless categorization.

Tech: Android Studio, Java/Kotlin, XML

E-Commerce

🛒 TrendCart & MMN Store

The Problem: Most small-business storefronts suffer from 3+ second load times and cluttered checkouts, leading to massive cart abandonment rates.

The Solution: I developed two high-performance retail platforms (TrendCart for general e-commerce and MMN Store as an official brand hub). By leveraging modern web frameworks, I achieved sub-second load times and a frictionless user journey designed explicitly to convert visitors into buyers.

Tech: React, Tailwind CSS, Stripe integration concepts

The MMN Ecosystem (2/2)

EdTech Platform

🎓 SkillzUp

The Problem: Quality tech education is scattered across the internet, making it inaccessible and confusing for absolute beginners.

The Solution: Built a centralized learning hub providing structured pathways. The focus was on an interactive, engaging UI that keeps students motivated to learn modern digital skills.

SaaS Utilities

🛠️ MyNTool

Instead of visiting 10 different websites for daily digital tasks (calculators, formatters), I built a "Swiss Army Knife" web platform. One fast-loading site for everyday problem-solving.

Web Gaming

🎮 GamesArenas

Browser games are usually hosted on spammy, heavy websites. I created a clean, immersive gaming arena using HTML5 Canvas where users can play directly with zero friction.

AI Engineering

🤖 Zain (The AI Assistant)

Beyond standard web apps, I integrated a custom AI Agent (Zain) into the MMN Codes agency platform. Utilizing OpenRouter and LLMs, Zain acts as a human-like executive assistant, captures leads, and routes them directly to my secure Admin Dashboard CRM.
