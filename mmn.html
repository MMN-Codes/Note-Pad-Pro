The Tech Stack

A premium product requires a premium foundation. I discarded bloated, legacy systems (like WordPress or basic PHP) and adopted the modern Serverless Edge Architecture used by companies like Vercel and Notion.

Data Flow & Security Model

1. Frontend (React/Vite)

Delivers 60fps animations, 3D tilt effects, and sub-second page loads.
